# LifeOs - Primary Theme Design

## Logo & Branding
**Ecosystem Name:** LifeOs
**Core Philosophy:** 
- The center represents **YOU**.
- The ring represents **LIFE**.
- The gap represents **THE PRESENT MOMENT**.
- The ongoing circle represents **ONGOING PROGRESS**.
**Brand Pillars:** Clean, timeless, focused (no distraction), and simple.

## Color Palette (High-Contrast Monochromatic)
- **Background:** Crisp White (`#FFFFFF`) or Deep Black (`#000000`) for dark mode.
- **Surface:** Off-white (`#F9F9F9`) or Dark Gray (`#111111`).
- **Primary:** Stark Black (`#000000`) or Pure White (`#FFFFFF`).
- **Secondary:** Neutral Gray (`#888888`) for less emphasized elements.
- **Text:** High contrast—black on white, or white on black.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Clean, geometric sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Helvetica Neue, or a stark geometric font like Plus Jakarta Sans) to match the circular geometry of the logo.
- **Body:** Highly legible sans-serif. High contrast and clean tracking.

## Core Components
- **Buttons:** Sharp edges or perfectly rounded pill shapes. Solid black with white text (or inverted). Minimalist borders.
- **Cards/Bento:** Subtle or no borders. Relies on negative space and stark contrast rather than heavy drop shadows.
- **Navigation:** Stripped back, using simple iconography and clean text. 

## Additional Notes
- The interface should feel like an operating system for life: utilitarian, focused, and free of visual clutter.
- Micro-animations should be smooth but fast, emphasizing efficiency and ongoing progress.
